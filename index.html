About

The philosophy at TKO Tennis is based on the turtle. A turtle can stay inside it's shell and be safe. But, it chooses to move, by sticking it's neck out. In that process, the turtle faces challenges, bravely discovers opportunities and continues to move forward. The turtle embodies bravery, exploration, patience and determination. We challenge and enable our students to bring on the turtle mentality, whether it be the tennis court, classroom or life. We encourage "sticking your neck out" and trying to be all you can be.

Leadership

TKO tennis is owned by Adrian Strong, a former high level USTA junior and NCAA Division 1 college tennis player. Growing up as one of the elite juniors from Indiana, Adrian traveled across the nation from a very young age competing in national level tournaments. He has played in elite NCAA tennis tournaments including Rolex invitational, NCAA Div 1 conference tournaments and regional and national events. As a coach, he works with some of USTA Southern elite juniors. He also works with several ALTA teams and provides group lessons to men and women of all skill levels. Adrian is supported by assistant pros, fitness staff and mental coaches.

Services

High Performance Lesson

Team TKO Tennis works with high performance juniors eyeing the prized college tennis scholarship, or a spot in the high school tennis team. Some students have in mind, a professional tennis career. These lessons cover all four aspects of tennis: technical, physical, mental and tactical. We work with the students in creating outcome-performance-process goals and work everyday to achieve them. Age ranges from 5 to adult players. High performance lessons can be private (1 pro to 1 student ) or semi private (1 pro to few students).

Tennis Bootcamp

Tennis at all levels have become a specialized fitness game. If you are not tennis fit, it's impossible to do well. Simple workouts in gym are not enough anymore. We have created specialized fitness training for tennis. Our boot camps are clubbed into three groups: Kids (5 to 12), Performance (built for the high performance player ages 12 and above), Adult recreational. We work on tennis specific speed, power, strength, flexibility and injury prevention exercises built for the particular group.
